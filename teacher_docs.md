# 🧑‍🏫 Teacher - Academic Operational Documentation

As a **Teacher**, you are the primary architect of the academic experience on the platform. Your role focuses on creating high-quality question banks and overseeing exam delivery for your students.

---

## 🗂️ Personal Question Bank
- **Bulk Upload**: Effortlessly import hundreds of questions using our standardized Excel template.
- **Manual Management**: View, search, and edit your uploaded questions to ensure accuracy.
- **Audit Logs**: Every question you create or update is logged for transparency and institution-wide consistency.
- **Cleanup**: Soft-delete outdated questions to maintain a relevant repository.

---

## 📝 Exam Creation & Delivery
- **Flexible Setup**: Create new exam sessions with custom titles, subjects, and durations.
- **Question Selection**: Pick specific questions from your personal bank to build targeted assessments.
- **Targeted Enrollment**: Select exactly which students are allowed to take each exam.
- **Proctoring Readiness**: Set up secure exam codes that students must use to enter the session.

---

## 📊 Result Monitoring & Analytics
- **Live Tracking**: Monitor student completion status for your exams.
- **Score Analysis**: View detailed student attempts and individual question responses.
- **Standardized Export**: Download exam results (Student ID, Name, Marks) in Excel format for institutional records.
- **Performance Evaluation**: Identify student strengths and weaknesses through centralized score reports.

---

## 🛡️ Operational Safety
- **Soft Deletion**: All your deletions (Questions, Exams) are soft-deletes, allowing the SAAS Admin to recover data in case of accidental removal.
- **Accountability**: Your actions contribute to the institution's audit trail, ensuring a secure and reliable testing environment.

---
> [!TIP]
> Use the **Excel Template** for bulk uploads to save time when migrating large question banks to the platform.
