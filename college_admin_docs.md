# 🏫 College Admin - Institutional Governance Documentation

As a **College Admin**, you have full operational control over your institution's specific portal. You are responsible for managing staff, students, and the local question bank to ensure smooth academic operations.

---

## 👨‍🏫 Staff (Teacher) Management
- **Teacher Onboarding**: Add new teachers to your institution.
- **Staff Oversight**: Monitor all active teachers within your college.
- **Soft Deletion**: Remove teachers from the active list (moves them to the system-wide trash for SAAS Admin review).

---

## 🎓 Student Management
- **Student Enrollment**: Create individual student accounts.
- **Global Student List**: View and manage all students registered under your institution.
- **Cleanup**: Soft-delete student records when necessary.
- **Export Data**: Download a full list of institutional students in Excel format for offline records.

---

## 📚 Question Bank & Exams
- **Institutional Repository**: Browse every question uploaded by your teachers.
- **Exam Orchestration**: Create and schedule exam sessions for your students.
- **Result Governance**: Control when students can see their marks by releasing results.
- **Exam Cleanup**: Soft-delete outdated or cancelled exam sessions.

---

## 📊 Analytics & Reporting
- **Live Dashboard**: Instant visibility into the total number of Teachers, Students, and Exams conducted.
- **Performance Tracking**: Access comprehensive results for any exam session.
- **Results Export**: Download detailed student performance reports (ID, Name, Marks) in a standardized Excel format.

---

## 🛡️ Safety & Accountability
- **Soft Delete Mechanism**: All deletions (Teachers, Students, Exams) are soft-deletes, ensuring data can be recovered by the SAAS Admin if needed.
- **Audit Compliance**: Every major action is tracked in the platform's central audit trail for accountability.

---
> [!NOTE]
> Unlike the SAAS Admin, College Admins cannot permanently delete data. All deleted items are sent to the Platform Trash for final review by the SAAS Admin.
