# 🎓 Student - Personalized Learning & Testing Documentation

As a **Student**, your portal is designed to provide a seamless testing experience and clear insights into your academic progress.

---

## 🚀 Exam Participation
- **Active Exams**: Browse all exams currently assigned to you by your teachers.
- **Secure Entry**: Access exams using unique session codes provided by your institution.
- **Testing Interface**: A distraction-free environment to answer MCQs with real-time timer tracking.
- **Automatic Submission**: Exams are automatically submitted when the timer runs out, ensuring no data loss.

---

## 🎯 Practice & Skill Building
- **Unlimited Practice**: (If enabled by your college) Access a massive pool of questions to sharpen your skills across various subjects.
- **Instant Correction**: Receive immediate feedback on practice questions to learn as you go.

---

## 📊 Performance & History
- **My Results**: View a comprehensive history of all your completed exams.
- **Detailed Analysis**: Breakdown of correct vs. incorrect answers to help you focus on weak areas.
- **Leaderboard**: Compete with peers and see where you rank within your institution based on score and speed.

---

## 👤 Profile & Preferences
- **Account Management**: View and update your personal details (Name, Email).
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your overall growth and exam consistency over time.

---
> [!IMPORTANT]
> Always ensure you have a stable internet connection before starting a timed exam. Result visibility depends on your College Admin releasing the marks.
